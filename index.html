How to use this website

To see the planes being mapped by my three receivers, click on the receiver of interest below on the left. Then click on the top link to the right to open a tab to that receivers output. For best results, adjust map so you can see most of Florida.

ADS-B East Takes you to the output map for this receiver

Receiver on second floor near east facing window.
  • Receiver: Flightaware Pro
  • Software: PIAware Rasbian Image
  • Computer: Raspberry PI 3B
  • Antenna: 1090MH Indoor Vertical
  • Frequency: 1090 MH (ADS-B ES)
  • Feeds: FlightAware Link to FlightAware aggregator I feed into. Here you will see the results of all the feeders.
  • Installed: 2/8/20
